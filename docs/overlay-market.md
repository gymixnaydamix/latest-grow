# Market Overlay App

## Overview
Market is the commerce overlay for listings, orders, sellers, and promotional offers.

## Metadata
- App ID: `market_overlay`
- Label: `Market`
- Category: `Commerce`
- Theme: `from-violet-500 to-purple-600`
- Description: `Marketplace, orders, sellers, and offers`

## Primary Navigation
### Marketplace
- `product_listings`: Product Listings
- `categories`: Categories
- `search`: Search
- `featured`: Featured

### Orders
- `order_management`: Order Management
- `order_history`: Order History
- `tracking`: Tracking
- `returns`: Returns

### Sellers
- `seller_profiles`: Seller Profiles
- `seller_verification`: Seller Verification
- `seller_analytics`: Seller Analytics
- `reviews`: Reviews

### Offers
- `discount_codes`: Discount Codes
- `promotions`: Promotions
- `special_offers`: Special Offers
- `bulk_pricing`: Bulk Pricing

## Default Entry
- Primary: `marketplace`
- Secondary: `product_listings`
